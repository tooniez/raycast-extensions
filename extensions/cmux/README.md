# cmux

A [Raycast](https://raycast.com) extension for [cmux](https://cmux.app), the terminal multiplexer for Mac.

## Features

This extension lets you control cmux without leaving your keyboard. Commands are available via Raycast to navigate and manage your workspaces and surfaces directly from anywhere on your Mac.

For example, you can search through your open workspaces and jump straight to one, or quickly focus a specific surface (tab) across any workspace — all without switching to cmux manually.

## Requirements

- [cmux](https://cmux.app) installed and running
- [Raycast](https://raycast.com) installed
