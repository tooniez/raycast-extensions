# Kagi News

Read and explore Kagi News articles and sources seamlessly in Raycast.

## Features

- Access all news categories (World, Technology, Science, etc.), automatically updated from the Kagi News API.  
- Article summaries include images, key points, perspectives, timelines, and more.  
- Personalize article summaries by activating or deactivating over 30 sections.  
- All sources for each story are provided and clickable.  
- The Time Travel command enables browsing the Kagi News archives for earlier dates.  
- A favorites system simplifies access to categories of interest.  
- Historical events and figures are featured in the Today in History category.  
- Supports more than 25 languages.

## Usage

- Open Raycast, type "Daily/Kagi News," select a category, and browse articles. 
- To access the archives, open the Time Travel command, enter a date, select a batch, and browse the articles.
- Use the settings to choose your preferred language and the sections you wish to display.
