# Shape Calendar

Search, create, and manage your training activities from [Shape Calendar](https://shapecalendar.com).

## Setup

1. Get your API token from [Shape Calendar Settings](https://shapecalendar.com/settings)
2. Open the extension preferences in Raycast and paste your token

## Commands

- **Search Activities** — Browse your planned and completed activities with a detail view
- **This Week** — See your training plan for the current week at a glance
- **Today's Workout** — Pin to root search to always see today's workout

## AI Extension

Ask Shape Calendar questions like:

- "What did I do this week?"
- "How was my last workout?"
- "Schedule a 10k run for tomorrow"
- "Create a training plan for next week"
- "Mark all my planned workouts as completed"
