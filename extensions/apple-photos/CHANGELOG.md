# Apple Photos Changelog

## [Initial Release] - 2026-03-12

### Added

- **Search Recent Photos** command: Browse your most recent Apple Photos in a 3-column grid
- **Paste Recent Photo** command: Paste your most recent photo directly into the frontmost app
- Progressive thumbnail loading — photos appear in order as they generate
- Copy to Clipboard action (primary, ↵)
- Paste to [App] action (⌘↵) — shows the name of the frontmost app
- Open in Photos action (⌘O)
- Quick Look preview (⌘Y)
- Refresh action (⌘R)
- Configurable photo count preference (default: 24)
- Thumbnail caching — subsequent opens are instant
