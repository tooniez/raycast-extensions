# Irish Rail Changelog

## [1.1.0] - 2026-03-09

### Added
- Support for intermediate destinations: trains now appear when your destination is any stop along the route, not just the final terminus
- Destination dropdown now filters to only show stations reachable from the selected origin

### Fixed
- ETA now shows arrival time at your selected destination, not the train's final terminus

## [Initial Version] - 2024-01-08
