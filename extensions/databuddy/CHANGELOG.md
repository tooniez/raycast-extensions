# Databuddy

## [Open Website Directly in Databuddy] - 2026-04-04

- Deep link website "Open in Databuddy" actions to the selected website page

## [Initial Version] - 2026-04-01

- View all websites connected to your Databuddy account
- Analytics overview with visitors, page views, sessions, bounce rate, and session duration
- Time-series chart showing visitors and page views over time
- Top pages, referrers (with favicons), and countries (with flags)
- Date range filtering (Today, Yesterday, Last 7/14/30/90 Days)
- Create, edit, and delete websites
- Toggle public dashboard visibility
- View all short links with click analytics
- Link analytics: clicks, referrers, countries, devices, and browsers
- Create, edit, and delete short links with custom slugs
- Feature flag management with status toggles and rollout controls
- Funnel analytics with step-by-step conversion and drop-off rates
