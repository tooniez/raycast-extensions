# Duden Dictionary Changelog

## [Initial Version] - 2026-04-18

### 🆕 Features
- Search for German words quickly from Raycast
- Get complete word information including:
  - Word with article (e.g., "Löffel, der")
  - Part of speech
  - How common the word is (shown with stars)
  - Word breakdown by syllables
  - Full definitions and meanings
  - Synonyms
  - Usage notes
  - Word origin
  - Pronunciation guide
  - Alternative spellings
  - Example sentences
- Smart features:
  - Instantly shows details when only one result is found
  - Fast, responsive search
  - Shows up to 12 most relevant results
- Quick actions to copy word information
- Open words directly on Duden.de website
- Works fast with smart caching
