# Privacy Policy — Days 2

**Last updated:** March 7, 2026

## What Days 2 does

Days 2 is a Raycast extension that displays countdowns to your all-day Google Calendar events. It runs locally on your computer.

## Data we access

Days 2 requests **read-only** access to your Google Calendar (`calendar.readonly` scope). Specifically, it reads:

- Your list of calendars (names, colors)
- All-day event data (title, date, description, link)

## How your data is used

- All data is processed **locally on your device** within the Raycast application
- Your calendar data is used solely to display event countdowns
- No data is sent to any external server, analytics service, or third party
- No data is stored outside of Raycast's local storage on your machine

## Data we do NOT collect

- No personal information is collected or transmitted
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No cookies or advertising identifiers
- No data sharing with third parties

## Authentication

Days 2 uses Google OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for authentication. Your Google credentials are handled entirely by Google's OAuth flow and Raycast's secure token storage. The extension never sees or stores your Google password.

## Data retention

You can revoke access at any time:

1. Go to [Google Account Permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
2. Find "Days 2" and click **Remove Access**

All locally cached data is removed when you uninstall the extension from Raycast.
